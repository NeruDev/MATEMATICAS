# Problemas de Integrales Impropias

---

## 5.1 Tipo I: Límites Infinitos

### Nivel Básico ★

1. $\displaystyle\int_1^{\infty} \frac{1}{x^2}\,dx$

2. $\displaystyle\int_0^{\infty} e^{-x}\,dx$

3. $\displaystyle\int_1^{\infty} \frac{1}{x^3}\,dx$

4. $\displaystyle\int_0^{\infty} e^{-2x}\,dx$

5. $\displaystyle\int_1^{\infty} \frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}\,dx$

### Nivel Intermedio ★★

6. $\displaystyle\int_0^{\infty} xe^{-x}\,dx$

7. $\displaystyle\int_1^{\infty} \frac{1}{x^2 + 1}\,dx$

8. $\displaystyle\int_0^{\infty} \frac{1}{(x+1)^2}\,dx$

9. $\displaystyle\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \frac{1}{1+x^2}\,dx$

10. $\displaystyle\int_0^{\infty} x^2 e^{-x}\,dx$

### Nivel Avanzado ★★★

11. $\displaystyle\int_0^{\infty} \frac{x}{(1+x^2)^2}\,dx$

12. $\displaystyle\int_1^{\infty} \frac{\ln x}{x^2}\,dx$

13. $\displaystyle\int_0^{\infty} e^{-x}\cos x\,dx$

---

## 5.2 Tipo II: Discontinuidades

### Nivel Básico ★

14. $\displaystyle\int_0^1 \frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}\,dx$

15. $\displaystyle\int_0^4 \frac{1}{\sqrt{4-x}}\,dx$

16. $\displaystyle\int_0^1 \frac{1}{x^{2/3}}\,dx$

### Nivel Intermedio ★★

17. $\displaystyle\int_0^1 \frac{1}{x}\,dx$

18. $\displaystyle\int_0^{\pi/2} \tan x\,dx$

19. $\displaystyle\int_0^1 \frac{\ln x}{\sqrt{x}}\,dx$

20. $\displaystyle\int_0^2 \frac{1}{\sqrt{2-x}}\,dx$

### Nivel Avanzado ★★★

21. $\displaystyle\int_0^3 \frac{1}{(x-1)^{2/3}}\,dx$

22. $\displaystyle\int_0^1 \frac{1}{\sqrt{x(1-x)}}\,dx$

---

## 5.3-5.4 Convergencia y Comparación

### Determinar convergencia o divergencia

23. $\displaystyle\int_1^{\infty} \frac{1}{x+1}\,dx$

24. $\displaystyle\int_1^{\infty} \frac{1}{\sqrt{x^3+1}}\,dx$

25. $\displaystyle\int_2^{\infty} \frac{1}{x\ln x}\,dx$

26. $\displaystyle\int_1^{\infty} \frac{\sin^2 x}{x^2}\,dx$

27. $\displaystyle\int_0^{\infty} \frac{1}{1+e^x}\,dx$

28. $\displaystyle\int_1^{\infty} \frac{x^2+1}{x^4+1}\,dx$

29. $\displaystyle\int_2^{\infty} \frac{1}{x(\ln x)^2}\,dx$

30. $\displaystyle\int_1^{\infty} \frac{\arctan x}{x^2}\,dx$

---

## 5.5 Integrales p

31. ¿Para qué valores de $p$ converge $\displaystyle\int_1^{\infty} \frac{1}{x^p}\,dx$?

32. ¿Para qué valores de $p$ converge $\displaystyle\int_0^{1} \frac{1}{x^p}\,dx$?

33. ¿Para qué valores de $p$ converge $\displaystyle\int_0^{\infty} \frac{1}{x^p}\,dx$?

34. Evaluar $\displaystyle\int_1^{\infty} \frac{1}{x^{5/4}}\,dx$.

35. Evaluar $\displaystyle\int_0^{1} \frac{1}{x^{3/4}}\,dx$.

---

## Problemas Mixtos

### Nivel Avanzado ★★★

36. $\displaystyle\int_0^{\infty} \frac{1}{\sqrt{x}(1+x)}\,dx$

37. $\displaystyle\int_1^{\infty} \frac{1}{x\sqrt{x^2-1}}\,dx$

38. Demostrar que $\displaystyle\int_0^{\infty} e^{-x^2}\,dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}$.
(Sugerencia: Considerar $\left(\int_0^{\infty} e^{-x^2}dx\right)^2$ en coordenadas polares)

39. Determinar si converge: $\displaystyle\int_0^{\infty} \frac{\sin x}{x}\,dx$

40. Evaluar $\displaystyle\int_0^1 x^p \ln x\,dx$ para $p > -1$.
